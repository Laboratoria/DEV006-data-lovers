Studio Ghibli, acclaimed Japanese animation studio was founded in 1985 by animators and directors Miyazaki Hayao, Takahata Isao and producer Suzuki Toshio. Studio Ghibli is known for the high quality of its filmmaking and its artistry. Its feature films have won both critical and popular praise and had also influenced other animation studios.
